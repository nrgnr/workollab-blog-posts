# Welcome to the Workollab blog

This site is where we publish updates about what Workollab is building, what we are learning from open source work, and what looks worth paying attention to across the tools and systems we use.

## What you can expect here

- Product and platform updates
- Notes from open source work
- Curated links we think are useful
- Short writeups explaining how we approach real technical problems

## Why we are doing this

Workollab is easier to understand when the work is visible. This blog is meant to make that work easier to follow, both for people close to the project and for anyone who wants practical context around what we are building.

## Subscribe

If you want updates in your inbox, use the subscribe form on the site. If you want to follow the writing only, just read along here.
